ABOUT HERITAGE HATS HISTORY

Heritage Hats offers quality custom made and brand name hats for customers in the Phoenix Scottsdale Cave Creek area and nationwide. From westerns and fedoras to Panamas, caps, and specialty styles, we carry a wide selection to fit every personality and lifestyle. We believe a hat is more than an accessory it's an expression of who you are and we're proud to help keep Arizona (and beyond) covered in style and comfort.
